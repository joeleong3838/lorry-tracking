PMW Lorry Tracking System - OSHES Management

1. Purpose & Use

This system is designed exclusively for tracking lorry movements at PMW facilities for Occupational Safety, Health, and Environmental (OSHES) compliance purposes.

2. Data Collection

The system collects and stores:

  • Driver names and contact information
  • Vehicle registration numbers
  • Company details
  • Entry and exit timestamps
  • Purpose of visit and location
  • Security guard information

3. Privacy & Confidentiality

All information collected is:

  • Used solely for security and operational purposes
  • Stored securely in compliance with data protection regulations
  • Accessible only to authorized PMW personnel
  • Not shared with third parties without consent

4. User Responsibilities

Guards and Operators must:

  • Enter accurate and truthful information
  • Verify driver identity before check-in
  • Maintain confidentiality of system access
  • Report any system issues immediately
  • Not tamper with or delete records
