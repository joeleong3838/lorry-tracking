Overview

Welcome to the PMW OSHES Lorry Tracking System. This system is designed to manage and monitor lorry movements within the premises for safety, security, and operational efficiency.

System Usage

By using this system, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information when logging lorry check-ins and check-outs
  • Use the system only for authorized purposes related to lorry tracking and management
  • Maintain the confidentiality of any sensitive information accessed through this system
  • Report any system issues, errors, or suspicious activities to the system administrator immediately

Data Privacy & Protection

We take data privacy seriously. All information entered into this system, including driver details, lorry information, and visit logs, is:

  • Stored securely in compliance with data protection regulations
  • Used solely for operational purposes related to site security and logistics management
  • Accessible only to authorized personnel
  • Retained in accordance with company data retention policies

User Responsibilities

As a user of this system, you are responsible for:

  • Ensuring all lorry check-ins are recorded accurately and promptly
  • Verifying driver and vehicle information before entry
  • Processing check-outs in a timely manner when lorries leave the premises
  • Following all OSHES (Occupational Safety, Health, Environment & Security) protocols
